HOW I GOT HERE

I’m currently a User Experience Design Apprentice at Fresh Tilled Soil in Watertown, MA. Passionate about user experience, interface design and all things digital, I’m looking forward to developing and growing as a UX Designer.

As a lover of typography, and someone who respects traditional design, my experience also includes logo development and brand identity creation, art direction, packaging and event collateral design (invitations/announcements).

My pre-design life encompasses a breadth of advertising and marketing roles, including media planning at large and mid-sized agencies and print, digital, mobile and event sales at some of the country’s top fashion and lifestyle publications with large national and international brands.

Past sales and media client categories include Fashion (Levi’s, Andrew Marc, Ann Taylor, Madewell), Healthcare (Airbourne, Botox, Juvederm), Liquor (Pernod Ricard- Absolut, Beefeater, Chivas, Stoli, Perrier Jouet, Glenlivet, Wild Turkey, Patron), Entertainment (VH1, Nickelodeon/Nick Jr), Automotive (Land Rover), and Financial (American Express, Mastercard).
